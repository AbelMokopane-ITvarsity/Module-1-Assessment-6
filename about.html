About us


We are an Company based in Randfontein, Gauteng South Africa. We provide back-up power and solar power products to re-sellers and end users.


Our Mission


Our mission is to tackle loadshedding by providing good quality affordable solar and back up power products to the South African and regional markets. We keep our cost of sales as low as possible and in turn pass those saving on to the customer. We strive to import directly from the manufacturer as many of our products as possible and cut out the middle man.
